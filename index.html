About

About Me

I'm a maker from Tasmania, Australia, focused on designing practical hardware for the 3D printing community.

My projects tend to focus on real problems—reliable filament handling, better cooling, easier servicing. I design with modularity in mind so things can be upgraded or fixed without major hassle.

All my designs are shared under open-source licenses. You're welcome to build, modify, or improve on them. Some projects also have licensed manufacturers if you'd prefer to buy ready-made parts.
